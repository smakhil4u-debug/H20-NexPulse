Welcome to H2ONEXPULSE

Please read these terms carefully before starting your subscription service. By using our application, you agree to comply with these rules.

1. Account Registration & Eligibility

To access the water delivery features provided by H2ONEXPULSE, you must register a valid account using an active mobile phone number. You are solely responsible for ensuring that all account details, credentials, and profile information remain updated and accurate.

2. Subscription Plans & Scheduled Deliveries

H2ONEXPULSE offers custom subscription tiers and scheduled options to fit your lifestyle requirements:

  • Deliveries are processed, fulfilled, and automated entirely based on the selection arrays specified in your active app calendar.
  • Users can seamlessly cycle between our premium water categories, including Daily H2O and Premium H2O packages.
  • Any modifications, pauses, or immediate cancellations to scheduled deliveries must be executed in the application before the designated daily calendar cutoff time to avoid automatic dispatch.

3. The Empty Jar Exchange Policy

Our commercial operations rely strictly on an exact container lifecycle network. By accepting delivery services from H2ONEXPULSE, you explicitly acknowledge and consent to the empty jar clause:

  • Customers must return a clean, undamaged empty water jar of the identical volume segment for every fresh water jar delivered by our logistics partners.
  • If an empty jar is unavailable, damaged, or unreturned at the designated moment of delivery, the customer shall be liable to immediately forfeit their established jar security deposit.
  • Additional delivery dispatches may be restricted or paused if the customer accumulates an unbalanced jar deficit or outstanding container debt.

4. Service Area & Location Deliverability

Service availability is determined strictly by defined geographic delivery sectors and municipal boundaries:

  • Orders and recurring deliveries will only execute if your structural address coordinates fall within active H2ONEXPULSE geo-coverage blocks.
  • The application reserves the absolute right to restrict checkouts, cancel active plans, or mark a user coordinate block as "Location non-deliverable" if access routes are blocked or fall outside operations.

5. App Wallet, Payments & Fees

All balances, secure transactions, and individual order invoices are managed directly using the integrated digital platform portal:

  • Charges for water orders and jar deposits are deducted systematically from your active H2ONEXPULSE wallet balance or processed via approved digital gateways.
  • Any balance discrepancies, refund applications, or billing queries must be raised with our Customer Support division within 7 business days of invoice compilation.
