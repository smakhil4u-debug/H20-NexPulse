Cancellation & Refunds

Our Service Commitment

At H2ONEXPULSE, we aim to provide reliable deliveries. Review our guidelines below regarding order adjustments, delivery limits, and refund processing.

1. Delivery Terms & Conditions

  • H2ONEXPULSE operates and delivers products exclusively within selected operational city zones.
  • All dispatches are subject to real-time stock and inventory availability. Large orders may occasionally be split across multiple delivery slots.
  • Customers are required to inspect products for sealing integrity, structural damage, or quantity discrepancies immediately upon delivery.
  • Multi-Story Delivery Rule: To ensure the safety of our logistics partners, if your building does not have a functional service elevator, deliveries will be completed at the main security gate or up to the 2nd floor maximum.
  • Force Majeure: H2ONEXPULSE is not liable for structural delays or fulfillment failures caused by extreme weather conditions, labor strikes, regulatory adjustments, or local technical network disruptions.

2. Delivery Turnaround Time (TAT)

  • Orders confirmed before 4:00 PM are scheduled for fulfillment on the next immediate working day. Orders completed after 4:00 PM will be processed for delivery on the day following the next working day.
  • Our standard timeline window for processing empty jar returns or collecting standalone containers runs between 7 to 10 active working days from the initial request log.

3. Return & Refund Policy

Water products are generally non-returnable once accepted. However, product replacement pipelines are initialized if verified issues occur:

  • Defective or physically compromised items will be replaced within 3 to 5 business days. Issues must be reported to support within 3 days of delivery alongside clear photo documentation.
  • Approved order cancellations or balance returns will be credited directly back to your original mode of payment or app wallet within 7 working days.

4. One-Time Order Adjustments

  • Users can seamlessly cancel or adjust a one-time order up to 4 hours after initial placement without penalty.
  • Any delivery disputes or complaints can be filed by contacting our support channel at support@h2onexpulse.com or calling our toll-free assistance line at 1800-XXX-XXXX between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM.

5. Subscriptions & Empty Jar Guidelines

  • Upon terminating a subscription tier, your remaining unserved prepaid balance will be calculated and refunded within 7 working days, minus any early-termination promotional discounts.
  • Jar Quality Enforcement: Empty jars returned during delivery cycles must be clean, unadulterated, and reusable. Jars previously used to store chemicals, oils, or non-potable liquids will be rejected, and the user will forfeit their corresponding container security deposit. No smell, oil, or chemical contamination is allowed under any circumstances.
